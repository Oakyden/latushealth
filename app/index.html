 Services

Helping businesses and their employees

  • Health and Safety LATUS Health offers specialist training advice and consultancy services in a wide range of Health and Safety aspects, with special focus on:

    - Identifying company needs

    - Promoting a positive health and safety culture

    - Manual handling

    - Developing and implementing training to meet company needs

    - Formulating and delivering tailored courses

    - Management systems

  • Health Surveillance LATUS Health offers specialist training advice and consultancy services in a wide range of Health and Safety aspects, with special focus on:

    - Audiometry (hearing)

    - Spirometry (lung function)

    - HAV (hand arm vibration)

    - DSE and eyesight

    - FBlood testing

    - Management systems

    - Skin

    - Pre-placement assessments

    - Drug alcohol testing
  • Management Referral The LATUS Health management referral service includes:

    - Illness report hotline

    - Day one absence reporting

    - Management advice line

    - Management referrals

    - Active case management

    - Return to work
  • Manual Handling This process can include:

    - Manual handling risk assessment

    - Ergonomic, biomechanical analysis of job roles to identify risk factors

    - Design of safe handling procedures and techniques

    - The filming and production of a company/facility/job role specific safe handling induction video

    - On site manual handling training courses and certification

    - Training and support of manual handling champions

    - “Train the Trainer” courses to ensure the sustainability of safe handling with new employees

    - Prehabilitation exercises to strengthen and protect areas of the body at risk in a specific job role e.g. back strengthening exercises to reduce the likelihood of a back injury

  • Employee Wellbeing Improving the health of your company will directly improve your bottom line. Currently your company will be losing 2-4% of total payroll as a result of sick days. Now add to that the loss in production of a person’s daily work not getting performed, the cost of temporary staff or the cost of overtime to cover the absence. Plus you have to consider the time and cost to your company of the HR department then dealing with the issue of an ill employee and the organisation of any subsequent cover needed. It quickly becomes evident that ill health is costing your company and that a small investment in improving the health of your workforce will produce a large return.

    All of our wellbeing programs are bespoke to individual companies. Contact us to discover how we can help improve your workforce.
