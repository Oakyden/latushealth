 Who we are

Helping businesses and their employees

LATUS Health is a team of health and wellbeing experts passionate about helping businesses and their employees perform at their best. We provide industry-leading health and wellbeing support programmes to a wide range of local and national businesses from our headquarters in East Yorkshire.

Our mission is to improve the performance of every company we work with as a direct result of us improving the mental and physical health of every employee.

 Services

Reducing sickness and absence, Preventing and removing health risks in the workplace, Screening and surveillance to detect early stages of ill health. Getting the right services in place can greatly improve the wellbeing of your employees and their working environment.

  • Health Surveillance


    We provide health surveillance to support your needs, whether that be as a sole occupational health provider, an ad hoc screening process or to supplement your current in house OH department. Screening can be carried out using your onsite facilities or we can supply the necessary facilities using one of our mobile health units.

  • Biological monitoring

    As part of your COSHH policy your company may need to implement some biological monitoring. We will guide you through the process of setting up the monitoring system, sample collection and result interpretation. Employees in contact with spray paints, glues, foams, resins, lead and many other substances may require some monitoring, so please contact us for advice.


  • Drug and Alcohol testing

    If your Drugs and Alcohol policy requires random testing of employees then contact us for information on how we can provide this service for you.

  • Absence management

    Early intervention is key to speeding up return to work times and quality advice and treatment reduces the likelihood of reoccurrence of the same injury or illness. Our management referral services include:

    - Day one absence reporting/recording
    - Referral to OH Advisor
    - Referral to OH Physician
    - Return to work programmes
    - Physiotherapy
    - Counselling

  • Medicals

    We provide medicals across a range of industries to ensure employees are fit and prepared for work:

    - Pre-employment

    - Drivers

    - Offshore

    - Travel

  • Immunisations and vaccinations

    We manage and administer immunisation programmes for organisations so they can:

    - protect employees and customers

    - comply with legislation and good practice

  • Manual handling

    - Manual handling risk assessment
    - Ergonomic, biomechanical analysis of job roles to identify risk factors
    - Design of safe handling procedures and techniques
    - The filming and production of a company/facility/job role specific safe handling induction video
    - On site manual handling training courses and certification
    - Training and support of manual handling champions

  • Wellbeing

    We build bespoke wellbeing programmes to engage employees in improving their mental and physical health using our industry-first wellbeing App.

    Each company is built differently so it is essential for the success of any wellbeing programme that each initiative is designed with the individual needs of the company and employees in mind. Our agile disruptive approach to employee wellbeing has set us aside as a Best-In-Class provider, helping us win contracts with some of the UK's best companies.

  • Health & Safety

    LATUS Health offers specialist training advice and consultancy services in a wide range of Health and Safety aspects, with special focus on:

    - Identifying company needs
    - Promoting a positive health and safety culture
    - Manual handling
    - Developing and implementing training to meet company needs
    - Formulating and delivering tailored courses
    - Management systems
